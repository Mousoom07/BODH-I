# 🧠 BODH-I

### AI-Powered Cognitive Stress Assessment & Intervention Platform

BODH-I is an intelligent mental wellness platform developed to assess, monitor, and improve cognitive and psychological well-being among young adults. The system integrates validated psychometric assessments, cognitive testing, explainable artificial intelligence, and evidence-based interventions within a unified digital environment.

The platform was developed as part of a research initiative focused on understanding the relationship between stress, cognition, sleep quality, and behavioral health outcomes in young adults.

---

## 🌟 Overview

BODH-I combines psychological assessment, cognitive evaluation, machine learning, and personalized interventions to create a comprehensive mental wellness ecosystem.

The platform enables users to:

* Assess stress, anxiety, and sleep quality
* Evaluate cognitive performance
* Receive AI-powered wellness insights
* Track longitudinal mental health trends
* Engage in guided intervention exercises
* Visualize brain-inspired cognitive analytics

## 🧠 Research Foundation

The platform is based on a longitudinal cognitive-stress assessment framework designed for young adults in India.

* 500+ Participants
* 42+ Variables
* 7 Longitudinal Assessment Periods
* Multi-Dimensional Wellness Indicators

* PSS
* STAI
* PSQI
